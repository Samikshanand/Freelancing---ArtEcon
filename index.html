What is BIM?

Building Information Modeling (BIM) is an intelligent 3D model-based process that gives architecture, engineering, and construction (AEC) professionals the insight and tools to more efficiently plan, design, construct, and manage buildings and infrastructure.
This technology is a digital transformation for AEC Industry and soon will become new normal. Now you can build your career at faster pace by learning and applying your skills on Large scale projects.

Career in BIM?

Career building in BIM is much promising and as this technology is saving time and cost of projects by 20%, it gets higher salaries (2x to 5x) than other Civil Engineering jobs.
We can help you to start your career in BIM ! Along with our highly experienced trainers, we assure great and in depth knowledge imparting so that you can boost your career.
